Who do I Think I am Anyway?

Hi! I'm Miguel. And I love computer science (when I'm not getting NullPointerExceptions). I strive for creative, elegant, and remarkable design in my work, while maintaining a healthy balance with efficiency.

I went to South River High School, where I built my knowledge base for STEM fields under Project Lead the Way, a program that guided me to graduation with varied fields of Engineering courses under my belt. Looking back, these courses and my demanding teachers instilled in me the virtues of patience and attention to detail that are now so imperative in the field of computer science.

I transferred to the University of Maryland intending to become an engineer. However, one thing led to another, and I eventually realized my passion for computer science. I am now enjoying new projects like developing Android applications, designing and implementing a database, and this website, which I designed mainly with HTML and CSS. I am graduating on May 2018 and I am currently a Corporate Technical Intern for an IT solutions company for the Spring 2017 semester.
